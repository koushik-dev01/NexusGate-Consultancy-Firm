International Forum November 2027 Doha, Qatar

Hosted in Doha, Qatar

A global hub for diplomacy, capital, and international partnerships, serving as a neutral and strategic platform for decision-makers to meet, exchange, and structure concrete projects.

Purpose of the Event

Africa Global Gateway aims to facilitate direct dialogue between African public authorities and international companies, unlock investment opportunities in key development sectors, accelerate partnerships between Africa and global infrastructure players, and transform strategic discussions into bankable and executable projects.

Why Qatar?

Qatar stands at the crossroads of Africa, Asia, and the Gulf, offering a trusted diplomatic environment, world-class infrastructure and connectivity, access to regional and international investors, and a neutral platform for high-level engagement.

Who Will Attend

  • African government representatives and public agencies
  • Asian and Gulf EPC companies and investors
  • Infrastructure, energy, water, housing, and digital solution providers
  • Financial institutions and development partners
  • Strategic advisors and project facilitators

Key Focus Areas

Infrastructure & Transport

Roads, railways, ports, airports, and logistics hubs.

Energy & Renewable Solutions

Solar, wind, grid modernization, and clean energy projects.

Water & Environmental Projects

Desalination, irrigation, wastewater treatment, and sustainability.

Industrial Development & Manufacturing

Factories, SEZs, industrial parks, and value-added processing.

Digital Transformation & Smart Cities

5G networks, smart governance, data centers, and IoT solutions.

Our Vision

Africa Global Gateway is more than an event. It is a gateway for long-term partnerships, sustainable development, and global cooperation focused on Africa's growth.
